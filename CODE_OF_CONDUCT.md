# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Vesper community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuroatypicality, physical appearance, body size, age, race, or religion
- Unwelcome sexual attention
- Deliberate misgendering or use of "dead" or rejected names
- Threats of violence or incitement to violence

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated fairly. All community members are expected to abide by this Code of Conduct.
